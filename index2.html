Welcome to St. Aloysius LifeTeen

Welcome to St. Aloysius LifeTeen in Hickory, NC. LifeTeen is an international Catholic movement that serves the Church by providing resources and faith experiences that help lead teens closer to Christ. We accomplish this through a vibrant Eucharistic spirituality and by creating opportunities for teens to grow in their faith.

Spring Retreat, March 13 - 15

Join us for our 2009 Spring Retreat, "In the Zone!" This year, St. Aloysius LifeTeen will be travelling to the Blowing Rock Conference Center in Blowing Rock, NC. Each year, we go on a faith-filled retreat to regain our focus on Christ and strengthen ourselves to the many spiritual attacks we face in every day life. The total cost of the retreat is $75, which includes transportation, lodging, food, and other miscellaneous items we hand out for the retreat. If you are interested, please contact Billy G by March 1 to reserve a spot!



March for Life 2009

This past January, St. Aloysius LifeTeen travelled to Washington, D.C. to protest the 1973 Supreme Court Decision of Roe v. Wade. We joined hundreds of thousands of other pro-life protestors and peacefully marched on the Capitol. St. Aloysius LifeTeen was also fortunate enough to meet with Congressman Patrick McHenry to encourage him to contineu with his pro-life voting record. This year, over 80 people from St. Aloysius and St. Joseph travelled to D.C. together.
